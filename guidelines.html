Guidelines

Rules and guidelines are part of every successful organisation. Please make sure you follow them.

Limit on number of bets on a person and window of betting

Each character you bet on will be available on for a specific window of time. Once that window passes, you are not able to bet on them.

There will also be a fixed count on the number of people who can place bets on one character. Once this number is full, betting on them will not be entertained.

High Demand, Lower Count of bets.

The higher the demand for a specific character, the numer of bets that can be placed on them will be restricted.

Bet for, bet against.

There are two options available to bet on a character. You can either bet on them to win or you can bet against them.
